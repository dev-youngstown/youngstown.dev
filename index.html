Our process

Each startup we work with is assigned a Project Manager - a senior software engineer who has years of experience building real-world software projects for companies of all sizes.

Each startup we work with is assigned a Project Manager - a senior software engineer who has years of experience building real-world software projects for companies of all sizes.

Our Project Managers help you create a well defined scope of work, perfect for a student to pick up. Our project managers are experts at cutting scope and work with a focus on getting your project into users hands as fast as possible.

We then use our connections with local organizations such as Code Youngstown, YSU, and MCCTC to select students are ready to take their experience to the next level, and are excited at the prospect of working on a project like yours.

Students execute the work transparently - expect weekly progress check-ins with your and to have access to all outputs from day 1.

Our Goals

  • Give talented local student software developers the 1 year of experience they need to be qualified to work remotely from Youngstown, OH
  • Continue to grow our community of local developers, Code Youngstown
  • Encourage our community of local software developers to start their own startups
  • Repeat

What we don't do

  • Brownfields

    We believe that brownfield projects require a Project Manager who is experienced with the existing project to effectively guide and get the most out of a student. Instead, we suggest hiring an intern directly, which are happy to help you find!

  • Give companies direct access to students

    The Project Manager plays the important role of keeping the project within a scope executable by the student, which is an important part of making sure the project output is something that you can deliver to your users.

  • Not pay our students

    All of our students are paid fairly by the hour. Every two weeks our students bill us, then Dev Youngstown bills you accordingly.

  • Make any money

    For now, all of our Project Managers our volunteers. Please be kind to them and be conscientious of their time.
